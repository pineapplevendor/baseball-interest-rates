Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Mike Fetters?

Mike Fetters is 61 years old.

When was Mike Fetters born?

Mike Fetters was born on December 19, 1964.

Where was Mike Fetters born?

Mike Fetters was born in Van Nuys, CA.

How tall is Mike Fetters?

Mike Fetters is 6-4 (193 cm) tall.

How much did Mike Fetters weigh when playing?

Mike Fetters weighed 200 lbs (90 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Mike Fetters play?

Mike Fetters played 16 seasons.

Is Mike Fetters in the Hall of Fame?

Mike Fetters has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Mike Fetters drafted?

Mike Fetters was drafted by the Los Angeles Dodgers in the 22nd round of the 1983 MLB June Amateur Draft from Iolani HS (Honolulu, HI) and the California Angels in the 1st round (27th) of the 1986 MLB June Amateur Draft from Pepperdine University (Malibu, CA).

What position did Mike Fetters play?

Mike Fetters was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Mike Fetters have?

Mike Fetters had 518 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Mike Fetters played for?

Mike Fetters played for 8 teams; the Anaheim Angels, Arizona Diamondbacks, Baltimore Orioles, Los Angeles Dodgers, Milwaukee Brewers, Minnesota Twins, Oakland Athletics and Pittsburgh Pirates.

When did Mike Fetters retire?

Mike Fetters last played in 2004.
