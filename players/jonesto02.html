How old is Todd Jones?

Todd Jones is 57 years old.

Todd Jones was born on April 24, 1968.

Todd Jones weighed 200 lbs (90 kg) when playing.

Todd Jones played 16 seasons.

Is Todd Jones in the Hall of Fame?

Todd Jones has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Todd Jones drafted?

Todd Jones was drafted by the New York Mets in the 41st round of the 1986 MLB June Amateur Draft from R.L. Osborne HS (Marietta, GA) and the Houston Astros in the 1st round (27th) of the 1989 MLB June Amateur Draft from Jacksonville State University (Jacksonville, AL).

Todd Jones was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Todd Jones have?

Todd Jones had 868 strikeouts over his career.

Todd Jones played for 8 teams; the Boston Red Sox, Cincinnati Reds, Colorado Rockies, Detroit Tigers, Florida Marlins, Houston Astros, Minnesota Twins and Philadelphia Phillies.

Todd Jones has never won a World Series.
