Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Jeff Francoeur?

Jeff Francoeur is 42 years old.

When was Jeff Francoeur born?

Jeff Francoeur was born on January 8, 1984.

Where was Jeff Francoeur born?

Jeff Francoeur was born in Atlanta, GA.

How tall is Jeff Francoeur?

Jeff Francoeur is 6-4 (193 cm) tall.

How much did Jeff Francoeur weigh when playing?

Jeff Francoeur weighed 225 lbs (102 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Jeff Francoeur play?

Jeff Francoeur played 12 seasons.

Is Jeff Francoeur in the Hall of Fame?

Jeff Francoeur has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Jeff Francoeur drafted?

Jeff Francoeur was drafted by the Atlanta Braves in the 1st round (23rd) of the 2002 MLB June Amateur Draft from Parkview HS (Lilburn, GA).

Jeff Francoeur was a Rightfielder.

How many hits did Jeff Francoeur have?

Jeff Francoeur had 1,373 hits over his career.

Jeff Francoeur had 160 home runs over his career.

Jeff Francoeur had a .261 average over his career.

Jeff Francoeur played for 8 teams; the Atlanta Braves, Miami Marlins, Kansas City Royals, New York Mets, Philadelphia Phillies, San Diego Padres, San Francisco Giants and Texas Rangers.

Jeff Francoeur has never won a World Series.
