Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Gary Sheffield?

Gary Sheffield is 57 years old.

When was Gary Sheffield born?

Gary Sheffield was born on November 18, 1968.

Where was Gary Sheffield born?

Gary Sheffield was born in Tampa, FL.

How tall is Gary Sheffield?

Gary Sheffield is 5-11 (180 cm) tall.

How much did Gary Sheffield weigh when playing?

Gary Sheffield weighed 190 lbs (86 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Gary Sheffield play?

Gary Sheffield played 22 seasons.

Is Gary Sheffield in the Hall of Fame?

Gary Sheffield has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Gary Sheffield drafted?

Gary Sheffield was drafted by the Milwaukee Brewers in the 1st round (6th) of the 1986 MLB June Amateur Draft from Hillsborough HS (Tampa, FL).

What position did Gary Sheffield play?

Gary Sheffield was a Rightfielder, Third Baseman and Shortstop.

How many hits did Gary Sheffield have?

Gary Sheffield had 2,689 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Gary Sheffield have?

Gary Sheffield had 509 home runs over his career.

What was Gary Sheffield's average?

Gary Sheffield had a .292 average over his career.

How many teams has Gary Sheffield played for?

Gary Sheffield played for 8 teams; the Atlanta Braves, Detroit Tigers, Florida Marlins, Los Angeles Dodgers, Milwaukee Brewers, New York Mets, New York Yankees and San Diego Padres.

How many World Series has Gary Sheffield won?

Gary Sheffield has won 1 World Series.

When did Gary Sheffield retire?

Gary Sheffield last played in 2009.

What are Gary Sheffield's nicknames?

Sheff is a nickname for Gary Sheffield.
