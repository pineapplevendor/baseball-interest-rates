When was Adam LaRoche drafted?

Adam LaRoche was drafted by the Florida Marlins in the 18th round of the 1998 MLB June Amateur Draft from Fort Scott HS (Fort Scott, KS), the Florida Marlins in the 42nd round of the 1999 MLB June Amateur Draft from Fort Scott Community College (Fort Scott, KS) and the Atlanta Braves in the 29th round of the 2000 MLB June Amateur Draft from Seminole State College (Seminole, OK).
