What position did Paul Lo Duca play?

Paul Lo Duca was a Catcher, First Baseman and Leftfielder.

How many hits did Paul Lo Duca have?

Paul Lo Duca had 1,112 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Paul Lo Duca have?

Paul Lo Duca had 80 home runs over his career.

What was Paul Lo Duca's average?

Paul Lo Duca had a .286 average over his career.

How many teams has Paul Lo Duca played for?

Paul Lo Duca played for 4 teams; the Florida Marlins, Los Angeles Dodgers, New York Mets and Washington Nationals.

How many World Series has Paul Lo Duca won?

Paul Lo Duca has never won a World Series.

When did Paul Lo Duca retire?

Paul Lo Duca last played in 2008.
