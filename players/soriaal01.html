Alfonso Soriano is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

Alfonso Soriano weighed 195 lbs (88 kg) when playing.

Alfonso Soriano played 16 seasons.

Is Alfonso Soriano in the Hall of Fame?

Alfonso Soriano has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.
