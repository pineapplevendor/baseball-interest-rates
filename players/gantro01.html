Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Ron Gant?

Ron Gant is 60 years old.

When was Ron Gant born?

Ron Gant was born on March 2, 1965.

Where was Ron Gant born?

Ron Gant was born in Victoria, TX.

How tall is Ron Gant?

Ron Gant is 6-0 (183 cm) tall.

How much did Ron Gant weigh when playing?

Ron Gant weighed 172 lbs (78 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Ron Gant play?

Ron Gant played 16 seasons.

Is Ron Gant in the Hall of Fame?

Ron Gant has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Ron Gant drafted?

Ron Gant was drafted by the Atlanta Braves in the 4th round of the 1983 MLB June Amateur Draft from Victoria HS (Victoria, TX).

What position did Ron Gant play?

Ron Gant was a Leftfielder, Second Baseman and Third Baseman.

How many hits did Ron Gant have?

Ron Gant had 1,651 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Ron Gant have?

Ron Gant had 321 home runs over his career.

What was Ron Gant's average?

Ron Gant had a .256 average over his career.

How many teams has Ron Gant played for?

Ron Gant played for 8 teams; the Anaheim Angels, Atlanta Braves, Cincinnati Reds, Colorado Rockies, Oakland Athletics, Philadelphia Phillies, San Diego Padres and St. Louis Cardinals.

How many World Series has Ron Gant won?

Ron Gant has never won a World Series.

When did Ron Gant retire?

Ron Gant last played in 2003.
