Is Kip Wells in the Hall of Fame?

Kip Wells has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Kip Wells drafted?

Kip Wells was drafted by the Milwaukee Brewers in the 58th round of the 1995 MLB June Amateur Draft from Lawrence E. Elkins HS (Missouri City, TX) and the Chicago White Sox in the 1st round (16th) of the 1998 MLB June Amateur Draft from Baylor University (Waco, TX).

Kip Wells was a Pitcher.
