Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Scott Kazmir?

Scott Kazmir is 42 years old.

When was Scott Kazmir born?

Scott Kazmir was born on January 24, 1984.

Where was Scott Kazmir born?

Scott Kazmir was born in Houston, TX.

How tall is Scott Kazmir?

Scott Kazmir is 6-0 (183 cm) tall.

How much did Scott Kazmir weigh when playing?

Scott Kazmir weighed 185 lbs (83 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Scott Kazmir play?

Scott Kazmir played 13 seasons.

Is Scott Kazmir in the Hall of Fame?

Scott Kazmir has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Scott Kazmir drafted?

Scott Kazmir was drafted by the New York Mets in the 1st round (15th) of the 2002 MLB June Amateur Draft from Cypress Falls HS (Houston, TX).

What position did Scott Kazmir play?

Scott Kazmir was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Scott Kazmir have?

Scott Kazmir had 1,618 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Scott Kazmir played for?

Scott Kazmir played for 7 teams; the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim, Cleveland Indians, Houston Astros, Los Angeles Dodgers, Oakland Athletics, San Francisco Giants and Tampa Bay Rays.

How many World Series has Scott Kazmir won?

Scott Kazmir has never won a World Series.

When did Scott Kazmir retire?

Scott Kazmir last played in 2021.

What are Scott Kazmir's nicknames?
