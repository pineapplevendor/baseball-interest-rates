John Mabry is 55 years old.

John Mabry was born on October 17, 1970.

John Mabry played 14 seasons.

John Mabry was drafted by the St. Louis Cardinals in the 6th round of the 1991 MLB June Amateur Draft from West Chester University of Pennsylvania (West Chester, PA).

John Mabry was an Outfielder, First Baseman and Pinch Hitter.
