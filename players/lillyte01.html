Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Ted Lilly?

Ted Lilly is 50 years old.

When was Ted Lilly born?

Ted Lilly was born on January 4, 1976.

Where was Ted Lilly born?

Ted Lilly was born in Lomita, CA.

How tall is Ted Lilly?

Ted Lilly is 6-0 (183 cm) tall.

How much did Ted Lilly weigh when playing?

Ted Lilly weighed 195 lbs (88 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Ted Lilly play?

Ted Lilly played 15 seasons.

Is Ted Lilly in the Hall of Fame?

Ted Lilly has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Ted Lilly drafted?

Ted Lilly was drafted by the Toronto Blue Jays in the 13th round of the 1995 MLB June Amateur Draft from Fresno City College (Fresno, CA) and the Los Angeles Dodgers in the 23rd round of the 1996 MLB June Amateur Draft from Fresno City College (Fresno, CA).

What position did Ted Lilly play?

Ted Lilly was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Ted Lilly have?

Ted Lilly had 1,681 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Ted Lilly played for?

Ted Lilly played for 6 teams; the Chicago Cubs, Los Angeles Dodgers, New York Yankees, Oakland Athletics, Toronto Blue Jays and Montreal Expos.

How many World Series has Ted Lilly won?

Ted Lilly has never won a World Series.

When did Ted Lilly retire?

Ted Lilly last played in 2013.
