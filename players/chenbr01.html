Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Bruce Chen?

Bruce Chen is 48 years old.

When was Bruce Chen born?

Bruce Chen was born on June 19, 1977.

Where was Bruce Chen born?

Bruce Chen was born in Panama, Panama.

How tall is Bruce Chen?

Bruce Chen is 6-2 (188 cm) tall.

How much did Bruce Chen weigh when playing?

Bruce Chen weighed 215 lbs (97 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Bruce Chen play?

Bruce Chen played 17 seasons.

Is Bruce Chen in the Hall of Fame?

Bruce Chen has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

Bruce Chen was a Pitcher.

When did Bruce Chen retire?

Bruce Chen last played in 2015.
