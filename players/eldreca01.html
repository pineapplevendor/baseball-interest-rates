Cal Eldred is 6-4 (193 cm) tall.

How much did Cal Eldred weigh when playing?

Cal Eldred weighed 215 lbs (97 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Cal Eldred play?

Cal Eldred played 14 seasons.

Is Cal Eldred in the Hall of Fame?

Cal Eldred has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Cal Eldred drafted?

Cal Eldred was drafted by the New York Mets in the 26th round of the 1986 MLB June Amateur Draft from Urbana Community HS (Center Point, IA) and the Milwaukee Brewers in the 1st round (17th) of the 1989 MLB June Amateur Draft from University of Iowa (Iowa City, IA).

What position did Cal Eldred play?

Cal Eldred was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Cal Eldred have?

Cal Eldred had 939 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Cal Eldred played for?

Cal Eldred played for 3 teams; the Chicago White Sox, Milwaukee Brewers and St. Louis Cardinals.

When did Cal Eldred retire?

Cal Eldred last played in 2005.
