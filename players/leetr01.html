How old is Travis Lee?

Travis Lee is 50 years old.

Travis Lee was born on May 26, 1975.

Where was Travis Lee born?

Travis Lee was born in San Diego, CA.

How tall is Travis Lee?

Travis Lee is 6-3 (190 cm) tall.

How much did Travis Lee weigh when playing?

Travis Lee weighed 205 lbs (92 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Travis Lee play?

Travis Lee played 9 seasons.

Is Travis Lee in the Hall of Fame?

Travis Lee has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Travis Lee drafted?

Travis Lee was drafted by the Minnesota Twins in the 1st round (2nd) of the 1996 MLB June Amateur Draft from San Diego State University (San Diego, CA).

How many hits did Travis Lee have?

Travis Lee had 958 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Travis Lee have?

Travis Lee had 115 home runs over his career.

What was Travis Lee's average?

Travis Lee had a .256 average over his career.

How many teams has Travis Lee played for?

Travis Lee played for 4 teams; the Arizona Diamondbacks, New York Yankees, Philadelphia Phillies and Tampa Bay Devil Rays.

Travis Lee last played in 2006.
