Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Ken Griffey Jr.?

Ken Griffey Jr. is 56 years old.

When was Ken Griffey Jr. born?

Ken Griffey Jr. was born on November 21, 1969.

Where was Ken Griffey Jr. born?

Ken Griffey Jr. was born in Donora, PA.

How tall is Ken Griffey Jr.?

Ken Griffey Jr. is 6-3 (190 cm) tall.

How much did Ken Griffey Jr. weigh when playing?

Ken Griffey Jr. weighed 195 lbs (88 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Ken Griffey Jr. play?

Ken Griffey Jr. played 22 seasons.

Is Ken Griffey Jr. in the Hall of Fame?

Ken Griffey Jr. was elected into the Hall of Fame in 2016.

When was Ken Griffey Jr. drafted?

Ken Griffey Jr. was drafted by the Seattle Mariners in the 1st round (1st) of the 1987 MLB June Amateur Draft from Archbishop Moeller HS (Cincinnati, OH).

What position did Ken Griffey Jr. play?

Ken Griffey Jr. was a Centerfielder.

How many hits did Ken Griffey Jr. have?

Ken Griffey Jr. had 2,781 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Ken Griffey Jr. have?

Ken Griffey Jr. had 630 home runs over his career.

What was Ken Griffey Jr.'s average?

Ken Griffey Jr. had a .284 average over his career.

How many teams has Ken Griffey Jr. played for?

Ken Griffey Jr. played for 3 teams; the Chicago White Sox, Cincinnati Reds and Seattle Mariners.

How many World Series has Ken Griffey Jr. won?

Ken Griffey Jr. has never won a World Series.

When did Ken Griffey Jr. retire?

Ken Griffey Jr. last played in 2010.

What are Ken Griffey Jr.'s nicknames?

Junior,The Kid,The Natural are nicknames for Ken Griffey Jr..
