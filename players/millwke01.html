Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Kevin Millwood?

Kevin Millwood is 51 years old.

When was Kevin Millwood born?

Kevin Millwood was born on December 24, 1974.

Where was Kevin Millwood born?

Kevin Millwood was born in Gastonia, NC.

How tall is Kevin Millwood?

Kevin Millwood is 6-4 (193 cm) tall.

How much did Kevin Millwood weigh when playing?

Kevin Millwood weighed 230 lbs (104 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Kevin Millwood play?

Kevin Millwood played 16 seasons.

Is Kevin Millwood in the Hall of Fame?

Kevin Millwood has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Kevin Millwood drafted?

Kevin Millwood was drafted by the Atlanta Braves in the 11th round of the 1993 MLB June Amateur Draft from Bessemer City HS (Bessemer City, NC).

What position did Kevin Millwood play?

Kevin Millwood was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Kevin Millwood have?

Kevin Millwood had 2,083 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Kevin Millwood played for?

Kevin Millwood played for 7 teams; the Atlanta Braves, Baltimore Orioles, Cleveland Indians, Colorado Rockies, Philadelphia Phillies, Seattle Mariners and Texas Rangers.

How many World Series has Kevin Millwood won?

Kevin Millwood has never won a World Series.

When did Kevin Millwood retire?

Kevin Millwood last played in 2012.
