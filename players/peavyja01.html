How old is Jake Peavy?

Jake Peavy is 44 years old.

When was Jake Peavy born?

Jake Peavy was born on May 31, 1981.

Where was Jake Peavy born?

Jake Peavy was born in Mobile, AL.

How tall is Jake Peavy?

Jake Peavy is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

How much did Jake Peavy weigh when playing?

Jake Peavy weighed 195 lbs (88 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Jake Peavy play?

Jake Peavy played 15 seasons.

Is Jake Peavy in the Hall of Fame?

Jake Peavy has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Jake Peavy drafted?

Jake Peavy was drafted by the San Diego Padres in the 15th round of the 1999 MLB June Amateur Draft from St. Paul's Episcopal School (Mobile, AL).

Jake Peavy was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Jake Peavy have?

Jake Peavy had 2,207 strikeouts over his career.

Jake Peavy played for 4 teams; the Boston Red Sox, Chicago White Sox, San Diego Padres and San Francisco Giants.

Jake Peavy has won 2 World Series.

Jake Peavy last played in 2016.
