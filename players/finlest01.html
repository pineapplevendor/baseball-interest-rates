Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Steve Finley?

Steve Finley is 60 years old.

When was Steve Finley born?

Steve Finley was born on March 12, 1965.

Where was Steve Finley born?

Steve Finley was born in Union City, TN.

How tall is Steve Finley?

Steve Finley is 6-2 (188 cm) tall.

How much did Steve Finley weigh when playing?

Steve Finley weighed 175 lbs (79 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Steve Finley play?

Steve Finley played 19 seasons.

Is Steve Finley in the Hall of Fame?

Steve Finley has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Steve Finley drafted?

Steve Finley was drafted by the Atlanta Braves in the 11th round of the 1986 MLB June Amateur Draft from Southern Illinois University Carbondale (Carbondale, IL) and the Baltimore Orioles in the 13th round of the 1987 MLB June Amateur Draft from Southern Illinois University Carbondale (Carbondale, IL).

What position did Steve Finley play?

Steve Finley was a Centerfielder.

How many hits did Steve Finley have?

Steve Finley had 2,548 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Steve Finley have?

Steve Finley had 304 home runs over his career.

What was Steve Finley's average?

Steve Finley had a .271 average over his career.

How many teams has Steve Finley played for?

Steve Finley played for 8 teams; the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim, Arizona Diamondbacks, Baltimore Orioles, Colorado Rockies, Houston Astros, Los Angeles Dodgers, San Diego Padres and San Francisco Giants.

How many World Series has Steve Finley won?

Steve Finley has won 1 World Series.

When did Steve Finley retire?

Steve Finley last played in 2007.
