Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Cliff Lee?

Cliff Lee is 47 years old.

When was Cliff Lee born?

Cliff Lee was born on August 30, 1978.

Where was Cliff Lee born?

Cliff Lee was born in Benton, AR.

How tall is Cliff Lee?

Cliff Lee is 6-3 (190 cm) tall.

How much did Cliff Lee weigh when playing?

Cliff Lee weighed 205 lbs (92 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Cliff Lee play?

Cliff Lee played 13 seasons.

Is Cliff Lee in the Hall of Fame?

Cliff Lee has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Cliff Lee drafted?

Cliff Lee was drafted by the Florida Marlins in the 8th round of the 1997 MLB June Amateur Draft from Benton HS (Benton, AR), the Baltimore Orioles in the 20th round of the 1998 MLB June Amateur Draft from Meridian Community College (Meridian, MS) and the Montreal Expos in the 4th round of the 2000 MLB June Amateur Draft from University of Arkansas (Fayetteville, AR).

What position did Cliff Lee play?

Cliff Lee was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Cliff Lee have?

Cliff Lee had 1,824 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Cliff Lee played for?

Cliff Lee played for 4 teams; the Cleveland Indians, Philadelphia Phillies, Seattle Mariners and Texas Rangers.

How many World Series has Cliff Lee won?

Cliff Lee has never won a World Series.

When did Cliff Lee retire?

Cliff Lee last played in 2014.
