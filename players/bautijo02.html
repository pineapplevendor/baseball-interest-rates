Frequently Asked Questions

How old is José Bautista?

José Bautista is 45 years old.

When was José Bautista born?

José Bautista was born on October 19, 1980.

Where was José Bautista born?

José Bautista was born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

How tall is José Bautista?

José Bautista is 6-0 (183 cm) tall.

How much did José Bautista weigh when playing?

José Bautista weighed 205 lbs (92 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did José Bautista play?

José Bautista played 15 seasons.

Is José Bautista in the Hall of Fame?

José Bautista has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was José Bautista drafted?

José Bautista was drafted by the Pittsburgh Pirates in the 20th round of the 2000 MLB June Amateur Draft from Chipola College (Marianna, FL).

What position did José Bautista play?

José Bautista was a Rightfielder and Third Baseman.

How many hits did José Bautista have?

José Bautista had 1,496 hits over his career.

How many home runs did José Bautista have?

José Bautista had 344 home runs over his career.

What was José Bautista's average?

José Bautista had a .247 average over his career.

How many teams has José Bautista played for?

José Bautista played for 8 teams; the Atlanta Braves, Baltimore Orioles, Kansas City Royals, New York Mets, Philadelphia Phillies, Pittsburgh Pirates, Tampa Bay Devil Rays and Toronto Blue Jays.

How many World Series has José Bautista won?

José Bautista has never won a World Series.

José Bautista last played in 2018.
