When was Mike Williams born?

Mike Williams was born on July 29, 1968.

Where was Mike Williams born?

Mike Williams was born in Radford, VA.

How tall is Mike Williams?

How much did Mike Williams weigh when playing?

Mike Williams weighed 190 lbs (86 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Mike Williams play?

Mike Williams played 12 seasons.

Is Mike Williams in the Hall of Fame?

Mike Williams has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Mike Williams drafted?

Mike Williams was drafted by the Philadelphia Phillies in the 14th round of the 1990 MLB June Amateur Draft from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Blacksburg, VA).

Mike Williams was a Pitcher.

Mike Williams had 584 strikeouts over his career.

Mike Williams has never won a World Series.

Mike Williams last played in 2003.
