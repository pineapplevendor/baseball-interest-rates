Mark Grudzielanek was drafted by the New York Mets in the 17th round of the 1989 MLB June Amateur Draft from J. M. Hanks HS (El Paso, TX) and the Montreal Expos in the 11th round of the 1991 MLB June Amateur Draft from Trinidad State College (Trinidad, CO).

Mark Grudzielanek was a Second Baseman and Shortstop.
