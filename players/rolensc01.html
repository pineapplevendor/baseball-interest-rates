Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Scott Rolen?

Scott Rolen is 50 years old.

When was Scott Rolen born?

Scott Rolen was born on April 4, 1975.

Where was Scott Rolen born?

Scott Rolen was born in Evansville, IN.

How tall is Scott Rolen?

Scott Rolen is 6-4 (193 cm) tall.

How much did Scott Rolen weigh when playing?

Scott Rolen weighed 245 lbs (111 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Scott Rolen play?

Scott Rolen played 17 seasons.

Is Scott Rolen in the Hall of Fame?

Scott Rolen was elected into the Hall of Fame in 2023.

When was Scott Rolen drafted?

Scott Rolen was drafted by the Philadelphia Phillies in the 2nd round of the 1993 MLB June Amateur Draft from Jasper HS (Jasper, IN).

What position did Scott Rolen play?

Scott Rolen was a Third Baseman.

How many hits did Scott Rolen have?

Scott Rolen had 2,077 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Scott Rolen have?

Scott Rolen had 316 home runs over his career.

What was Scott Rolen's average?

Scott Rolen had a .281 average over his career.

How many teams has Scott Rolen played for?

Scott Rolen played for 4 teams; the Cincinnati Reds, Philadelphia Phillies, St. Louis Cardinals and Toronto Blue Jays.

How many World Series has Scott Rolen won?

Scott Rolen has won 1 World Series.

When did Scott Rolen retire?

Scott Rolen last played in 2012.
