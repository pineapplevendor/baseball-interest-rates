Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Brad Penny?

Brad Penny is 47 years old.

When was Brad Penny born?

Brad Penny was born on May 24, 1978.

Where was Brad Penny born?

Brad Penny was born in Blackwell, OK.

How tall is Brad Penny?

Brad Penny is 6-4 (193 cm) tall.

How much did Brad Penny weigh when playing?

Brad Penny weighed 270 lbs (122 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Brad Penny play?

Brad Penny played 14 seasons.

Is Brad Penny in the Hall of Fame?

Brad Penny has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Brad Penny drafted?

Brad Penny was drafted by the Arizona Diamondbacks in the 5th round of the 1996 MLB June Amateur Draft from Broken Arrow HS (Broken Arrow, OK).

What position did Brad Penny play?

Brad Penny was a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts did Brad Penny have?

Brad Penny had 1,273 strikeouts over his career.

How many teams has Brad Penny played for?

Brad Penny played for 6 teams; the Boston Red Sox, Detroit Tigers, Miami Marlins, Los Angeles Dodgers, San Francisco Giants and St. Louis Cardinals.

How many World Series has Brad Penny won?

Brad Penny has won 1 World Series.

When did Brad Penny retire?

Brad Penny last played in 2014.
