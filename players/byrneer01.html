How old is Eric Byrnes?

Eric Byrnes is 50 years old.

Eric Byrnes weighed 200 lbs (90 kg) when playing.

Eric Byrnes played 11 seasons.

Is Eric Byrnes in the Hall of Fame?

Eric Byrnes has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Eric Byrnes drafted?

Eric Byrnes was drafted by the Los Angeles Dodgers in the 38th round of the 1994 MLB June Amateur Draft from St. Francis HS (Mountain View, CA), the Houston Astros in the 4th round of the 1997 MLB June Amateur Draft from University of California, Los Angeles (Los Angeles, CA) and the Oakland Athletics in the 8th round of the 1998 MLB June Amateur Draft from University of California, Los Angeles (Los Angeles, CA).

Eric Byrnes was an Outfielder.

How many hits did Eric Byrnes have?

Eric Byrnes had 827 hits over his career.

Eric Byrnes had 109 home runs over his career.

Eric Byrnes had a .258 average over his career.

Eric Byrnes played for 5 teams; the Arizona Diamondbacks, Baltimore Orioles, Colorado Rockies, Oakland Athletics and Seattle Mariners.

Eric Byrnes last played in 2010.
